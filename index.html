About Coffee and Grind

"Passion, precision, and people — the heart of Coffee and Grind."

We are a Black-owned, family-led café dedicated to uplifting our community. We believe in treating our employees like family, fostering a space where passion, respect, and purpose are felt in every interaction.

Our mission is to provide not just exceptional coffee, but a sanctuary where people can connect, create, and feel valued.
